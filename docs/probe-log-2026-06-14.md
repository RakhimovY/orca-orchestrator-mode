So v0.7.0 silently broke worker-mode's own invariant.

- verify-gate + self-correct: PRESENT (emergent) - the worker had a verify phase and fixed a red unauth test itself without bouncing to the coordinator.

The orchestrator's handoff ("carry A's key_decisions[] to dependent B") and drift ("compare the merged key_decisions[]") would have had nothing to carry → reconstruct from the diff → defeats N2/N7/N5.
- **impl-log: GAP** - the worker wrote NOTHING to the tracker during work ("final comment only", line 41), contradicting orchestrator-mode 0.7.0's mandated impl-log.
